Semi-annually, the Univeristy of Toronto publishes a magazine that is entirely written and designed by its student body. The Growing Issue is an exploration of growing, evolving, development, and change.

I acted as Designer Director for the magazine's graphics, layout, and typesetting. I also authored five poems featured inside.
